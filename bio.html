Bio

Viridiana Alcaraz Alvarez, born 2000, is a Bay Area based digital media artist. Initially, creating through traditional painting, Viridiana blends this tactile process within the digital world. One of her primary goals is to challenge the notion that digital art is sterile or lifeless. She showcases humanity behind the screen—that the digital space, like the physical, can carry warmth, emotion, and connection. You will find personal memories intertwined with cultural references, symbols, characters and text to create a dialogue between the past and present. Viridiana uses the digital space as a storage port that holds memories of childhood and loss.

Viridiana is currently studying at San Jose State University and will be receiving a BFA in Digital Media Art.
